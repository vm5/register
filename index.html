GRE 2024-25

IMPORTANT INFORMATION

Read the below instructions carefully, before filling up the online application form. You are advised to take a printout of these instructions to refer to the same while filling out the online application form:


  1. Apply online:
    • Apply exclusively through the official GRE Testing Portal website.
    • GRE (2024-25) will be conducted three times. Last date to apply for one mock or all the mocks is 11th August, 2024.

  2. Application Form submission:
    • First REGISTER to obtain a system-generated, unique, 6-digit Application Number (or User ID) beginning with "GRE" and Password. Note down this number for future correspondence.
    • Use a valid Email-ID and Mobile Number, which must remain active until the admission process gets completed. Application Number and Password will be sent to the registered email address and mobile number.
    • Ensure accuracy in providing Name, Date of Birth, Mobile Number, Email Address, and Gender, as these details cannot be modified post-registration.

  3. Form Printout:
    • Upon completing the online form and payment, it is recommended to take a printout for future reference.

  4. GRE(2024-25) Participation:
    • A candidate may appear a maximum of three times.
    • GRE(2024-25) will be held in three levels: L1 (17th August, 2024 to 18th August, 2024), L2 (5th October, 2024 to 6th October, 2024) and L3 (2nd November, 2024 to 3rd Novemeber, 2024).
    • A candidate may opt for L1 or ALL. A candidate may opt to appear thrice, even at the time of the first application. For a candidate who has appeared thrice in GRE (2024-25), the higher of the three scores will be considered for admission.
    • A candidate may also choose to appear the second time (i.e. L2) after the first appearance. To facilitate this process, candidates who have appeared during L1 but had not originally exercised the option to appear twice will be allowed to apply for L2 during and the same for L3 during the allotted dates that can be viewed on the website itself.
    • Candidates who did not register during (7th July, 2024 to 11th August, 2024) can register for only L2 and L3 during the allotted dates that can be viewed on the website itself.

  5. Application deadline:
    • The last date to register for GRE(2024-25) (L1 or all of them) is 11th August, 2024.
    • Submit the online form with the prescribed fee by 5:00 PM on the deadline. No need to send the form by post.

  6. Payment information
    • Payment information is not captured by ETS server.

  7. Additional note:
    • The pattern of the tests remains the same, i.e., QR+VR distributed in 4 sets with a total of 54 questions and 1 mark each.
    • The structure for AWA also remains the same, i.e., Analysis of an issue for 30 minutes.
    • The total time duration for QR+VR, however, is being increased to 88 minutes from 84 minutes.
    • The test can be taken at any time frame for the allotted time between 7 AM and 11 PM (both sections).
    • You are advised not to believe in fake calls, emails, posts, and SMS making false promises to help candidates clear the GRE.
    • Applications for Mock-2 only will open during the dates that will be intimated via the website.
    • All the updates regarding the admission process will be posted on the original website.
    • Click here to see all the important dates related to the admission process.
